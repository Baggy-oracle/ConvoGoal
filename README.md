# ConvoGoal
Convogoal helps you steer conversations to clear goals, actions, and results.
Convogoal is a goal-driven conversation utility that helps you decide what to say next in a conversation based on context, tone, and intent.
Instead of chatting with an AI, you paste a conversation, choose your goal, and get focused next-text suggestions that move the conversation toward your desired outcome in any language
